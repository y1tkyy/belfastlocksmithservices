Cookies Policy for Belfast Locksmith Services

At Belfast Locksmith Services, protecting your privacy is our priority. This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them on our website (www.belfastlocksmithservices.co.uk), and how you can manage them to suit your preferences.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) to enhance your browsing experience. They perform various functions, such as remembering your preferences and enabling website features.

Types of Cookies We Use

  1. Essential Cookies: These cookies are indispensable for the proper functioning of our website. Without them, certain features or services you rely on may not work.
    • Example: Cookies that maintain your login session.
  2. Performance and Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us analyse website performance and understand how visitors interact with different pages. The data collected is anonymous and used to improve our services.
    • Example: Google Analytics cookies that provide insights into visitor behaviour.
  3. Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow the website to remember your choices, such as language settings or preferred contact methods, enhancing your overall experience.
    • Example: Cookies that save your region or language preferences.
  4. Advertising and Targeting Cookies: These cookies may be used to deliver advertisements that are more relevant to you, based on your browsing habits.
    • Example: Cookies from third-party advertisers to tailor promotions.

Why We Use Cookies

Our use of cookies is aimed at:

  • Ensuring the website operates efficiently.
  • Gaining insights to improve user experience.
  • Delivering content tailored to your interests.
  • Enhancing security and detecting potential fraudulent activity.

Third-Party Cookies

We partner with trusted third-party services to enhance our website's functionality and reach. These include:

  • Google Analytics: To track and report website traffic and usage patterns.
  • Social Media Platforms: To enable content sharing and social interactions.
  • Advertising Networks: To display targeted advertisements.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party providers for more information on their practices.
